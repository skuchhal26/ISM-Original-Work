Important Safety Rules

  • Never share passwords, OTPs, bank details, or Social Security Number over email.
  • Do not send gift cards or money just because an email asks for it.
  • Even if the sender looks familiar, carefully check the full email address.
  • Modern phishing emails may have perfect grammar because attackers use AI tools.
  • This tool cannot directly inspect PDF, Word, or ZIP attachments, so attachments need extra caution.
